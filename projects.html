DelDOT Bridge Design Competition

May 2026

As lead designer on a three-person team, I created the CAD drawings for a tied-arch (bowstring) bridge and made sure the design held to strict competition specs — 14 inches long, under 4.5 inches wide, built only from the provided materials. I helped construct the final bridge and iterated on the design using bridge-design software and physical prototypes to fix weak points, like a vertical beam that couldn't handle the compressive force between the first and second arch. Our design won the competition.
